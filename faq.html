Audience, brands, press, and contact.

Concise answers for journalists, brand teams, and curious humans.

Who is Kilmaru?

Kilmaru is an anonymous US-based cinematic content creator. The face is never shown, and there is no spoken dialogue in the videos. Kilmaru has been active since 2021 and is best known for first-person footage of the world's most iconic cities, filmed as if humanity had vanished overnight. The work sits between social media content and short-form film, and has been described in press as solitude exploration or the empty world aesthetic.

What is Kilmaru's audience size and where is it located?

Kilmaru has 5.2 million followers combined across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. Content has accumulated over 2 billion views across all platforms. The audience is mostly in the United States and Europe. Average engagement rate is 6.2 percent.

What kind of content does Kilmaru create?

Kilmaru creates cinematic first-person video. Each piece shows a familiar location — a metropolis at night, a luxury hotel, an airport terminal, a cruise ship corridor, an iconic stadium — completely empty of other people. The hood stays up, the face stays out of frame, and there is no dialogue. Atmospheric sound and slow movement carry the storytelling. The content is platform-native (vertical, short-form) but built with the framing and patience of a short film. Press has called it the empty world aesthetic and solitude exploration.

How do I work with Kilmaru on a brand deal?

For brand partnerships, send a brief through the contact form or email hi@kilmaru.com. The full media kit includes audience demographics and partnership scenes. Past collaborations span automotive, audio, fashion, hospitality, travel, and entertainment categories. Initial outreach typically includes campaign goal, target market, timing window, and budget range.

What platforms is Kilmaru on?

Kilmaru publishes on TikTok at tiktok.com/@kilmaru, Instagram at instagram.com/kilmaruig, and YouTube at youtube.com/@kilmaruyt. Long-form work also lives at book.kilmaru.com. The combined audience across all platforms is 5.2 million followers.

What brands has Kilmaru worked with?

Past brand partnerships include Netflix, Disney, 20th Century, Mandarin Oriental, Insta360, Virgin Voyages, and the Empire State Building, among others. Integrations are typically format-led: the brand or product appears within a fully cinematic empty-world scene rather than as a traditional product placement, so the partnership reads as part of the content rather than an interruption to it.

Where has Kilmaru been featured in press?

Kilmaru has been featured in Wired Italia, Newsweek, Corriere della Sera, The Sun, Yahoo News, Benzinga, Daily Dot, Snopes, US Times Post, 20 Minutes (France), Fanpage, Geopop, Lo Speciale, and Monaco Life. The work has been analyzed in long-form by Corridor Crew (10M+ subscribers) and Night Mind. Kilmaru is a winner of the Monaco Streaming Film Festival. Direct links to coverage are on the media page.

What languages does Kilmaru work in?

Kilmaru's content has no spoken dialogue, so it travels across languages without translation. Communication for brand partnerships and press inquiries is handled in English.

How do I download the media kit?

The full media kit is published as a public page at kilmaru.com/media-kit.html. It includes follower counts, audience demographics by age, gender, and geography, engagement metrics, partnership scene examples, and selected press. There is no separate PDF download — the page itself is the press kit and can be shared by URL or printed directly from the browser.

How do I contact Kilmaru?

For brand partnerships, press inquiries, and other professional outreach, use the contact form or email hi@kilmaru.com. Direct messages on social platforms are not the most reliable way to reach Kilmaru's team.
