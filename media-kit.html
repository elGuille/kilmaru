The story

What if you woke up,
and everyone was gone?

Kilmaru is a cinematic content creator who explores the world's most iconic cities — completely empty. Times Square with no crowds. Shibuya Crossing in silence. Airports, hotels, stadiums, cruise ships: abandoned, as if humanity had simply vanished overnight.

Filmed in first person, always hooded, never showing a face, with no dialogue. Just atmospheric sound and a haunting sense of solitude. The result is a genre-defining body of work that sits somewhere between social media and short film.

Since 2021, Kilmaru has been pioneering what is now known as solitude exploration — a style that resonates across every culture and language, precisely because it has no language at all.

The scenes

Brand integrations
filmed as cinema.

Scene 01

The last drive

Ext. Downtown — Night

A solitary luxury car glides through the empty streets of a sleeping metropolis. No traffic, no pedestrians, no other headlights. Only the engine, echoing between skyscrapers. Kilmaru behind the wheel, hooded, silent.

Fit: automotive · luxury cars · urban transportation

Scene 02

Sound of silence

Int. International Airport — Dawn

Footsteps through an empty terminal. A moving walkway with no one on it. Kilmaru lifts a pair of headphones. Music breaks the vacuum. The contrast between absolute silence and premium sound — felt, not explained.

Fit: audio · headphones · airlines · airport retail

Scene 03

Empty runway

Int. Luxury Arcade — Day

A grand shopping arcade, every store open, not a single person inside. Every step frames the sneakers, the jacket, the silhouette. No crowd to compete with. The clothing holds the shot on its own.

Fit: fashion · sneakers · luxury retail

Scene 04

The last guest

Int. Five-Star Hotel — Continuous

A hotel with every light still on and not a soul behind the desk. Corridors, a lit pool, a rooftop with a view no one else is watching. The camera moves slowly. The brand isn't in the frame — it is the frame.

Fit: hospitality · hotels · tourism boards · cruise lines
