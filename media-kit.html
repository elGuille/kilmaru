01 — The Story

What if you woke up
and everyone was gone?

Kilmaru is a cinematic content creator who explores the world's most iconic cities — completely empty. Times Square with no crowds. Shibuya Crossing in silence. Airports, hotels, stadiums, entire metropolises — abandoned, as if humanity simply vanished overnight.

Filmed in first person, always hooded, never showing a face, with no dialogue — just atmospheric sound design and a haunting sense of solitude. The result is a genre-defining body of work that blurs the line between social media content and short film.

Pioneering what's now known as "solitude exploration," Kilmaru has built one of the most visually distinctive presences on the internet, with content that resonates across every culture, language, and demographic.

CONCEPT 01

The Last Drive

Automotive

A luxury car navigates the empty streets of a major city. No traffic. No pedestrians. Only the sound of the engine echoing between skyscrapers. Kilmaru behind the wheel.

Ideal for: Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Porsche, Genesis, Hyundai
🎧
CONCEPT 02

Sound of Silence

Audio · Technology

Walking through a deserted airport in absolute silence. Headphones on. Music fills the void. The contrast between total silence and premium sound — felt, not explained.

Ideal for: Sony, Bose, Bang & Olufsen, Apple AirPods Max

CONCEPT 03

Empty Runway

Fashion · Streetwear

Exploring a luxury shopping district — completely deserted. Every step showcases the product. No competition for attention. The clothing speaks for itself in a world of one.

The Last Guest
